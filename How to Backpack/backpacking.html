How to Backpack!

General Procedure

  1. Find a place to backpack, I generally use AllTrails but similar apps would work
  2. Figure out if you need a permit to backpack in this area, usually can find easily online
  3. Create a list of what you need to pack in your backpack
  4. Come up with simple, light, easy meals to eat
  5. Make sure you have plenty of water or ability to make clean water with a filter, boiling, or other method
  6. Once you are backpacking, make sure you find a place to set up your tent or sleep that is out of the elements
  7. Make sure your food is stored away from your campsite, preferably high in a tree out on a tree limb
  8. Enjoy your backpacking trip!

Tips and Tricks

  • Make sure you have properly researched the trail to understand distance, elevation gain, composition of terrain, and weather in that area (I have learned the hard way a few times)
  • Try your best to stay dry in cold weather because it's very hard to get warm when wet
  • Only pack the essentials and things you need to save weight, your back will thank you
  • Work your way up towards harder and longer backpacking trips
  • Make sure you let loved ones or freinds know where you're going in case something goes wrong, another option is having a satellite device
